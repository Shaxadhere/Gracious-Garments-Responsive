About Us


Pakistan’s 1ST FASHION MARKETPLACE where fashion enthusiasts come together to BUY AND SELL designer brands, labels and accessories from TOP DESIGNERS AND BRANDS. Shopping for your clothes or trying to find the right outfit for yourself has never been easier! By bringing a host of brands and designers to the table, Gracious Garments ensures that anything and everything your wardrobe needs is a click away! Quality listings and good reputation are very important to us. We ensure that you only see the best national and international brands on our platform.

GraciousGarments.TK is Pakistan’s first fashion marketplace which aims to increase the buying power of general consumer by making available some of the best brands available today. Think of it as your own personal shopping mall!We’re on a mission to bring trust and fairness to social entrepreneurship in online fashion industry in Pakistan, by making fashion brands at affordable rates and accessible to everyone.We validate users, verify listed items, and handle shipping and returns. We make reverse commerce as easy, authentic and reliable as e-commerce in Pakistan.

We display items from some of the best brands and designers in the business – whether they would be international or national. But we realise that not everyone is a shopaholic so we tailor these products to every individual’s needs. GraciousGarments.TK also encourages new fashion and design start-ups and individuals to set up shop with us and help grow their business through one platform. And one thing is certainly guaranteed, you won’t find a better place to shop from your favourite brands anywhere else.
